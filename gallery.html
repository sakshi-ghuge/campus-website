NavGurukul Pune campus Information

NavGurukul, founded in 2016 by Harvard and IIT-Delhi graduates, is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to providing access to aspirational careers for marginalized students. Their flagship fully funded 18-month residential program offers courses in programming, finance, business, and design. With seven centers across India, NavGurukul currently empowers over 1,145 students, having placed 830+ in tech and non-tech roles. Their unique educational model combines modern and traditional learning. Additionally, digital initiatives like Meraki and Zuvy extend their reach, offering online skills training and degrees with a focus on software industry careers.
